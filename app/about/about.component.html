ABOUT FLEET MANAGEMENT




                  Fleet management software is an integrated system of modules that keeps track of all business operations, vehicle maintenance, and fleet performance. Along with workflow automation, the system carries out business analysis, advances decision-making, and outlines prospects. The increasing adoption of digital means drives the considerable growth of the global fleet management market. According to the statistics, it was valued at $13.7 billion in 2017 and expected to increase to $28.6 billion by 2022. The diverse technology choice like cloud solutions, big data analytics, machine learning algorithms has brought fleet monitoring to the next level.

Today’s market is full of choices thus companies have to make several important decisions before they utilize fleet management solutions. After defining the needs and requirements, they need to know how to choose the right fleet management software. Such options as off-the-shelf and custom fleet management tools have their own pros and cons. Even though each type of system is beneficial for fleet control, they should be aware of the following details.

Fleet management solutions have become a common practice for transportation businesses. Even though they provide different types of services like delivering goods, car renting, or public transportation, the main purpose of applying fleet management software will remain the same. Managing activities and conditions of vehicles has been proved rather efficient, convenient, and more productive with the help of digital means. Along with improved daily operations, companies aim at the optimization of fleet performance and the increase in service quality. Understanding the company’s needs helps to outline the required software functionality as well as obtain the distinct advantages of implementing it.
